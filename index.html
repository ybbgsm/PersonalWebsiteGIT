Welcome to My Webpage

    I am Suiming Guo, a PhD student in Department of Information Engineering, the Chinese University of Hong Kong. My supervisor is Prof. Dah Ming Chiu. I obtained my B.S and M.S degree from Department of Electronic Engineering, Tsinghua University, in 2011 and 2013 respectively.
    I worked with Prof. Xing Li in Tsinghua University from 2011 to 2013 and focused in the research and development of high-quality distributed videoconferencing system. Last year (2012) I took an internship in Microsoft Asia&Pacific Research and Development Group (MSARD), and was involved in  the design and implementation of the product test infrastructure, as well as in code coverage improvement. In 2012, I also worked in CERNET (China Education and Research Network) and helped with designing and implementing a videoconferencing system.
